Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I edit my account?
    Enter your id in the user page, then click on the 'edit' button next to specific field.
  • How to create a new category?
    You can create a new category from the user's id page or by selecting the category bar from the menu.
  • How to delete a recent expenditure?
    Enter the category's id in which you saved your expenses. Select the expenditure you would like to delete, then click the delete button.
